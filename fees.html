Insurance & Payment

Out-of-network provider

Real Mind Psychotherapy is currently an out-of-network provider. This means we do not bill insurance directly — however, many clients are able to receive partial reimbursement through their out-of-network benefits.

We provide a monthly superbill (detailed receipt) that you can submit to your insurance company for potential reimbursement.

Why private pay?

Choosing private pay gives you and your therapist full control over your care — without insurance limitations on session frequency, duration, or diagnosis requirements.
