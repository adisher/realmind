Coverage

Insurance & Benefits

📋

Extended Health Benefits

Many employer benefit plans cover registered social workers (RSW) and registered psychologists (C.Psych). Check your plan for "psychotherapy" or "social work" coverage.

🧾

Superbill Receipts

We provide detailed receipts (superbills) after each session that you can submit to your insurer for reimbursement. We do not direct-bill most plans at this time.

🏥

Employee Assistance Programs (EAP)

Some EAP plans cover sessions with registered clinicians. Contact your EAP provider to confirm coverage before booking.

💰

Tax Deductibility

Psychotherapy fees paid to a registered clinician may qualify as a medical expense for Canadian tax purposes. Consult your accountant for details.
