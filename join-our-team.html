Who We're Looking For

Clinicians Who Lead with Curiosity

Clinically Excellent

You are committed to evidence-based practice and pursue ongoing learning — not because you have to, but because you want to.

Relationally Skilled

You understand that the therapeutic relationship is not a backdrop to the work — it is the work. You build trust with precision and warmth.

Culturally Humble

You approach every client's context with genuine curiosity, awareness of power, and a commitment to learning what you don't know.

Faith-Sensitive

You can work respectfully with clients for whom faith is central — or peripheral, or absent — without imposing your own framework.

Minimum Requirements

  • Active registration with OCSWSSW, CPO, or CRPO (Ontario)
  • Liability insurance through OASW, OPA, or equivalent
  • Clear criminal record check (within 6 months)
  • Commitment to ongoing clinical supervision and consultation

We also welcome applications from limited permit holders and practicum students seeking a placement. See below for details.
