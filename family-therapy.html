Systems-Based Approach

How We Work With Families

01

Family Systems Assessment

We map the family's patterns, roles, rules, and communication styles — understanding how each member's behaviour makes sense within the broader family system.

02

Individual & Joint Sessions

Depending on the presenting concern, we may meet with the full family, subsystems (parents only, siblings only), or individuals — creating a flexible structure that serves the work.

03

Communication & Boundary Work

We help families establish clearer communication norms, healthier boundaries, and more effective ways to repair ruptures when they occur.

04

Strengthening the Family Unit

We move beyond crisis resolution to help families build genuine cohesion — a shared identity, resilient rituals, and the capacity to navigate future challenges together.

What Families Gain

  • Reduced conflict and tension at home
  • Parents and children feeling heard and understood
  • Clearer roles and healthier boundaries
  • Support through grief, loss, or major transitions
  • Adolescents with stronger emotional regulation
  • Improved co-parenting after separation or divorce
  • A shared language for navigating hard moments
  • A stronger, more connected family unit
Faith & Culture

Honouring Your Family's Values & Heritage

Family life is deeply shaped by culture, tradition, and faith. Our therapists approach every family with genuine curiosity about what values and beliefs hold them together — and how those can become a resource in therapy.

We are particularly experienced in supporting immigrant families navigating generational and cultural tensions, and families where faith plays a central role in daily life.
