Glad Uko-Ima

LMHC-D, Clinical Supervisor | Real Mind Psychotherapy

Glad Uko-Ima is a New York State Licensed Mental Health Counselor and Clinical Supervisor, and the founder of Real Mind Psychotherapy. She holds a Master of Arts in Mental Health Counseling & Wellness from New York University, where she received advanced training in evidence-based therapeutic approaches and holistic mental health care.

She created her practice with one goal in mind: to offer a space where you can feel understood, supported, and guided toward real, lasting change.

Glad works with individuals who are navigating emotional challenges, life transitions, and relationship concerns, helping them make sense of their experiences and move forward with greater clarity and confidence. Her approach is thoughtful and collaborative — blending practical tools with deeper insight so you can not only cope, but truly grow.

She integrates evidence-based therapy with a holistic perspective that honors both the psychological and spiritual aspects of healing. Whether you're feeling overwhelmed, stuck, or simply ready for something different, Glad provides a grounded and supportive environment to help you reconnect with yourself and create meaningful change.

Associate Psychotherapist

Dr. Uko-Ima

Ph.D, LLM, MA, MFT-LP | Real Mind Psychotherapy

Dr. Uko-Ima is an Associate Psychotherapist at Real Mind Psychotherapy. He provides a highly personalized, discreet, and results-driven therapeutic experience for individuals, couples, and families seeking meaningful and lasting change.

He holds a Ph.D. in Forensic Psychology and practices under a Limited Permit in Marriage and Family Therapy, bringing a strong clinical and analytical foundation to his work.

He works with high-performing professionals, entrepreneurs, and individuals navigating complex emotional, relational, and life demands. Many of his clients are accustomed to functioning at a high level externally, yet experience internal stress, overthinking, relationship strain, or a persistent sense of imbalance. His work is designed to address these challenges with precision, depth, and intention.

Clinical Philosophy

His clinical philosophy is centered on the development of a "Real Mind" — a refined awareness of one's internal processes, enabling clarity in thinking, emotional regulation, and effective decision-making. This approach moves beyond surface-level coping, focusing instead on identifying and restructuring the underlying patterns that shape behavior, relationships, and overall well-being.

Couples Work

He is particularly sought after for his work with couples. He provides a structured and strategic approach to resolving communication breakdown, emotional disconnection, and trust-related concerns. His work helps partners move out of reactive cycles and into intentional, secure, and emotionally attuned relationships. Sessions are focused, outcome-oriented, and tailored to the unique dynamics of each couple.

Individual Work

In individual work, Dr. Uko-Ima integrates evidence-based modalities to deliver a comprehensive and sophisticated treatment experience. Clients gain not only insight, but also practical tools that support sustained growth and high-level functioning.

Faith Integration

For those who value faith, Dr. Uko-Ima offers the option of integrating Biblical principles of mind renewal into the therapeutic process. This approach is implemented with clinical precision and respect, supporting alignment between thought patterns, personal values, and relational life.

Dr. Uko-Ima's style is direct, thoughtful, and highly attuned. He maintains a balance of support and challenge, creating a space where clients are encouraged to engage deeply, take ownership of their growth, and achieve measurable outcomes.

As a clinician, Dr. Uko-Ima ensures that each client experience reflects a high standard of care — marked by professionalism, discretion, and a commitment to excellence.
