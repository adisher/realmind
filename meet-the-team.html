Founder & Lead Therapist

Glad Uko-Ima

MSW, RSW — Registered Social Worker

Background

Glad Uko-Ima is a Registered Social Worker with over a decade of experience in community mental health, hospital-based care, and private practice. She founded Real Mind Psychotherapy out of a conviction that every person deserves therapy that is both clinically rigorous and deeply human.

Clinical Approach

Glad integrates evidence-based modalities — including Gottman Method, EMDR, CBT, and somatic approaches — with a culturally informed, faith-sensitive lens. Her work is particularly shaped by years of supporting Black and racialized communities navigate mental health through a lens that truly understands their lived experience.

Staff Therapist

Dr. Uko-Ima

PhD, C.Psych — Registered Psychologist

Background

Dr. Uko-Ima holds a doctorate in Clinical Psychology and brings extensive training in evidence-based treatments for trauma, mood disorders, and relationship distress. With experience spanning academic medical centres and private practice, she combines intellectual rigour with genuine warmth in every therapeutic encounter.

Clinical Approach

Dr. Uko-Ima's approach is grounded in Cognitive Processing Therapy (CPT), Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), and emotionally focused frameworks. She works with clients to develop insight, build psychological flexibility, and achieve lasting change — not simply symptom reduction.
