Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Real Mind Psychotherapy ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of all individuals who visit our website or engage with our services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information in accordance with the Personal Health Information Protection Act (PHIPA) and the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

By using our website or contacting us, you agree to the terms described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number provided through our contact form
  • Inquiry details: information you voluntarily share about your reasons for seeking therapy
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages (collected automatically via analytics tools)
  • Employment information: details submitted through our "Join Our Team" application form

We do not collect sensitive personal health information through our website. Clinical records are maintained separately and governed by PHIPA.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and schedule consultations
  • Match you with the most appropriate clinician for your needs
  • Process employment and practicum applications
  • Improve the performance and content of our website
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Confidentiality of Clinical Information

All clinical communications and records are protected by professional confidentiality obligations under PHIPA and the ethical standards of the Ontario College of Social Workers and Social Service Workers (OCSWSSW) and the College of Psychologists of Ontario (CPO).

Information shared in therapy sessions will not be disclosed without your written consent, except in limited circumstances required by law (such as imminent risk of harm or mandatory reporting obligations).

5. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools (such as Vercel Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our site. This data is aggregated and anonymized — it does not identify you personally.

You may disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Doing so may affect the functionality of certain features on our website.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our website and communications:

  • Vercel: website hosting and deployment
  • Resend: transactional email delivery for form submissions

These services are bound by their own privacy policies and are used solely for the purposes described above. We do not share personal health information with these providers.

7. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and application data for a maximum of 12 months, after which they are securely deleted. Clinical records are retained according to the timelines required by provincial law and college standards.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address below.

9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. All data transmitted through our website is encrypted via HTTPS.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
