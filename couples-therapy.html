Gottman-Informed Approach

How We Work With Couples

01

Couples Assessment

We begin with both a joint session and individual sessions, creating a complete picture of each partner's perspective, history, and goals — without triangulation.

02

Understanding the Dynamic

Using Gottman's Sound Relationship House model, we identify the specific patterns — criticism, contempt, defensiveness, stonewalling — that are eroding connection.

03

Skill Building & Repair

We equip both partners with concrete communication tools, emotional regulation strategies, and repair techniques that work in real-life conflict.

04

Deepening Connection

Beyond crisis management, we help couples rebuild friendship, shared meaning, and the kind of deep knowing that makes a relationship truly secure.

What Couples Achieve

  • Breaking the cycle of recurring arguments
  • Restored emotional and physical intimacy
  • Clearer, safer communication patterns
  • Processed betrayal and rebuilt trust

  • Tools for navigating conflict constructively
  • A shared vision for the relationship's future
